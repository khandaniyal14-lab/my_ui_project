Africa House Pakistan

Africa House Pakistan is a national-level platform designed to strengthen Pakistan’s economic diplomacy, trade footprint, and investment presence across the African continent. We work hand-in-hand with government institutions, chambers, exporters, and policymakers to expand Pakistan’s access to Africa’s high-potential markets.

OUR VISION: To institutionalize Pakistan’s long-term economic presence in Africa through sustainable exports, strategic joint ventures, and innovation-driven collaborations..

OUR MISSION: To become the leading national gateway for Pakistan’s trade, investment, and policy engagement with Africa, while fostering economic empowerment, trust, and mutual growth..
